# Brindlewick Town Environment Pack

This pack is a coherent 3/4 JRPG town kit for Lanternhouse. Source art should be modern 16-bit JRPG pixel art with crisp 1px outlines, transparent backgrounds, readable color clusters, and no anti-aliasing.

Perspective rule: town, dungeon, and interior assets use top-down 3/4 JRPG exploration perspective on a rectangular 16x16 grid. Buildings are south-facing/front-facing facades: roof depth recedes upward, the front facade sits along the bottom, side hints are subtle, and the visible door aligns to a walkable 16x16 entrance threshold. Do not use isometric art, diagonal diamond-grid perspective, flat top-down icons, or standalone front-facing stamps.

Note: the first PixelLab building batch is integrated as a useful quality pass, but some outputs lean more isometric than the final target. Corrected rectangular-grid replacements are being generated and should supersede any diagonal-looking pieces.

Reference direction: classic SNES/PS1 JRPG towns with blue-gray cobblestone streets, raised grass plaza islands, red clay roofs, white plaster/stone facades, balconies, arched windows, flower boxes, curbs, and small stair/threshold connectors. Use these as modular town building blocks rather than one-off set pieces.
